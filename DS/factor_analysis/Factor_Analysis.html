Train Model

  • Simplified Loadings: Rotation aims to simplify the factor structure, making high loadings higher and low loadings lower
  • Orthogonal Rotation: Factors remain uncorrelated (e.g., Varimax)
  • Oblique Rotation: Factors are allowed to correlate (e.g., Promax)
In[6]:
from sklearn.decomposition import FactorAnalysis

model = FactorAnalysis(rotation="varimax", n_components=2, random_state=0)
model.fit(X)

Result Interpretation

  • Factor loadings represent the correlations between the observed variables (y-axis) and the latent factors (x-axis)
  • High Loadings, observed variables and factors have strong association (typically >0.4 or < -0.4)
  • Sign of Loadings, the sign (positive or negative) indicates the direction of the relationship

Interpretation

  • Interpreting Factors, a factor can be interpreted by looking at which observed variables have high loadings on it
  • sepal length, petal length, and petal width have high loadings with factor_1, which may represent flower volumn
  • sepal width have high loading with factor_2, which may represent sepal volumn

Communalities

  • Indicate the proportion of each observed variables variance that can be explained by the factors

  • $0.986^{2} + (-0.06)^{2} = 0.976$

  • High Communalities, sepal length, petal length, and petal width have communality above 0.925, suggesting it is well-represented by the two factors

  • Moderate Communality, sepal width has low comunalities, suggesting they are moderately well-represented by the two factors
