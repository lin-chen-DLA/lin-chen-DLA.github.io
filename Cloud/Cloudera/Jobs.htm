Jobs
  • A job automates the action of launching an engine, running a script, and tracking the results
  • Support *.py files
  • Does not support *.ipynb files
    • Schedule
  • Manual, manually click Run to implement the script
  • Recurring, run the job in a recurring pattern defined with a cron expression

  • Dependent, build a pipeline of jobs
    • The first job can be manual or recurring
    • The depedent job depends on the completion of its parent job
    • Each dependent job has one parent job
    • Cron Expression
    # cron expression
[minute] [hour] [day-of-month] [month] [day-of-week] command

# *, all

# ?, any, neglect the field value

# -, range

# , specifies multiple values

# / increments

# L, last

# W, weekday

# #, N-th occurrence of a weekday of the month
		
    0 12 * * * # at 12:00 every day

0/15 * * * * # every 10 minutes

0/15 13,18 * * * # 13:00, 13:15, 13:30, 13:45, 18:00, 18:15, 18:30, 18:45

0-5 13 * * * # every minute starting at 1 p.m. and ending at 1:05 p.m., every day

15,45 13 * 6 Tue # 13:15 and 13:45 every Tuesday in June

30 9 * * MON-FRI # 9:30 every Monday, Tuesday, Wednesdday, Thursday, and Friday

30 9 15 * * # 9:30 on the 15th of every month

0 18 L * * # 18:00 on the last day of every month

0 18 L-3 * * # 18:00 on the third day to last dday of every month

30 10 * * 5L # 10:30 on last Thursday of every month

0 10 * * 2#3 # 10:00 on third Monday of every month
